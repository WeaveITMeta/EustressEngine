# Eustress Manufacturing Program — Overview

**The world's first AI-driven, self-funding manufacturing network for hardware inventors.**

---

## What It Is

Eustress Manufacturing is a **zero-upfront-cost manufacturing program** that connects hardware inventors with vetted manufacturers and investors to bring physical products from idea to market in 12 weeks.

Unlike traditional manufacturing sourcing (months of cold emails, price negotiations, MOQ battles, and quality gambles), Eustress Manufacturing uses **AI allocation** to instantly match your product with the single best manufacturer and the minimum investor set needed to fund your pilot batch.

**No manufacturing experience required. No capital required. No supply chain expertise required.**

You bring the idea. We bring the factory, the funding, and the fulfillment.

---

## How It Works

### For Inventors (DIY Builders → Startups → Enterprises)

1. **Describe your product** in natural language via the Eustress Workshop chat panel
   - "I want to build a solid-state sodium-sulfur battery cell"
   - AI generates patent draft, SOTA validation, requirements doc, 3D mesh, BOM

2. **AI proposes a manufacturing deal** with equity split and royalty terms
   - Inventor: 60% equity + 5% royalty on net sales
   - Manufacturing Program: 25% equity + 8% royalty (funds future pilots)
   - Logistics Partner: 10% equity
   - Reserve Pool: 5% equity (future co-investors, advisors)

3. **AI allocates manufacturer + investors** in <5 seconds
   - Searches 500+ manufacturers across 40 countries
   - Scores on capability match (40%), quality (25%), cost (20%), speed (10%), risk (5%)
   - Selects the single best manufacturer for your entire BOM assembly
   - Finds minimum investor set (prefer 1 investor) whose combined check covers pilot capital

4. **Manufacturer reviews proposal** via web portal or email
   - Accept / Reject / Counter-Proposal (adjust price, lead time, MOQ)
   - Average response time: 18 hours

5. **Investor wires capital** (typically $10k–$250k for 1,000-unit pilot)
   - No dilution beyond the agreed equity split
   - No board seats, no pro-rata rights (unless investor requires it)

6. **Legal review** (patent filing, IP clearance, compliance check)
   - Automated for conventional products (<24 hours)
   - Manual review for exotic propulsion or regulated categories (3–5 days)

7. **Manufacturing begins** (30–45 day lead time typical)
   - Real-time status updates via WebTransport (no polling, instant push notifications)
   - Quality inspection at factory before shipment

8. **Warehousing + fulfillment** (3PL partners in US, EU, Asia)
   - Units delivered to nearest warehouse node
   - Listed in Eustress Products catalog
   - Integrated with Shopify, WooCommerce, or custom storefront

9. **Sales + royalties** (8% to fund, 5% to inventor, rest is gross margin)
   - Royalties auto-calculated on every unit shipped
   - Fund balance increases → next cohort of pilots funded
   - **Self-funding flywheel**: successful products fund new inventors

## FAQ

**Q: What if the manufacturer delivers defective units?**  
A: Quality inspection happens before shipment. If defect rate >3% over 90 days, manufacturer is auto-suspended and product is reallocated to backup manufacturer. Inventor is not charged for defective units.

**Q: What if my product doesn't sell?**  
A: You owe nothing. Investors take the loss on their equity stake. Manufacturing Program takes the loss on its 25% equity. You keep your 60% equity (worth $0 if no sales, but no debt).

**Q: Can I buy out the royalty later?**  
A: Yes. Royalty buyout formula: `(Annual Revenue × Royalty %) × 5-year multiplier`. Example: $100k annual revenue, 5% royalty = $5k/year royalty. Buyout = $5k × 5 = $25k lump sum.

**Q: Can I use my own manufacturer?**  
A: Yes, but they must join the network and pass the audit. If they're already vetted, AI will score them alongside other candidates. If they win on capability/cost/quality, they'll be allocated.

**Q: What if I need 100,000 units, not 1,000?**  
A: AI allocation works at any scale. For 100k+ units, you'll likely get multiple investors (2–5) and possibly a dedicated production line. Deal structure is negotiable for large orders.

**Q: Is this only for hardware? What about software?**  
A: Eustress Manufacturing is hardware-focused. For software, use traditional VC funding or bootstrapping. However, if your software controls a hardware product (e.g., firmware for a smart thermostat), the manufacturing program can fund the hardware and you retain 100% of the software IP.

**Q: What countries do you manufacture in?**  
A: 40+ countries across Asia (China, Taiwan, South Korea, Japan, Vietnam, India), Europe (Germany, Poland, Czech Republic, Italy), North America (US, Mexico, Canada), and South America (Brazil). AI selects based on capability match, cost, and lead time.

**Q: How do you prevent IP theft?**  
A: (1) Patent filing before manufacturer contact. (2) NDA signed by manufacturer before receiving BOM. (3) Manufacturers are audited and rated on IP compliance. (4) Legal recourse via international arbitration if theft occurs. (5) Eustress Manufacturing holds escrow of design files; manufacturer receives only production specs, not full CAD.

**Q: What if the AI picks the wrong manufacturer?**  
A: You can reject the allocation and request manual sourcing (adds 1–2 weeks). AI confidence score is shown; if <40%, manual review is auto-triggered. You can also override and select a different manufacturer from the ranked list.

**Q: Do I need to be an accredited investor to invest?**  
A: In the US, yes (SEC Regulation D). Outside the US, local securities laws apply. Eustress Manufacturing handles compliance per jurisdiction.

## Technical Details

### AI Allocation Algorithm

```
Score = (0.40 × Capability) + (0.25 × Quality) + (0.20 × Cost) + (0.10 × Speed) + (0.05 × Risk)

Capability = Jaccard similarity of (required processes ∩ manufacturer processes)
Quality = f(defect_rate, on_time_delivery_rate, audit_score)
Cost = 1 - |manufacturer_price - target_cost| / target_cost
Speed = 1 - (lead_time_days / pilot_deadline_days)
Risk = f(NCR_count, capacity_headroom, country_diversification)
```

Manufacturers scored 0–100. Top 5 ranked. Confidence = `top_score / (top_score + second_score)`.

If confidence <40%, escalate to manual review.

### Data Sources

- **Manufacturer registry**: 500+ manufacturers, updated weekly via TOML files
- **Investor registry**: 200+ investors, updated monthly via TOML files
- **Quality metrics**: Updated after every order (defect rate, on-time delivery, NCR count)
- **Pricing tiers**: Updated quarterly by manufacturers via web portal
- **Certifications**: Verified annually via third-party audits

### API Endpoints

```
GET  /api/v1/manufacturers          # Search manufacturers
GET  /api/v1/investors              # Search investors
POST /api/v1/deals                  # Propose deal
GET  /api/v1/deals/{id}             # Get deal status
GET  /api/v1/deals/{id}/timeline    # Get event timeline
PATCH /api/v1/deals/{id}            # Update deal (manufacturer/investor action)
```

All responses are JSON-LD with Schema.org markup for LLM parsing.

### Real-Time Updates

WebTransport protocol (QUIC-based) for bidirectional streaming:
- Server pushes: `status_change`, `timeline_event`, `action_required`
- Client subscribes: `subscribe`, `action_response`
- Sub-100ms latency, multiplexed streams, 0-RTT reconnection
